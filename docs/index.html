Hey there 🌵

My name is Alonso. I am an NLP specialist working in human language technologies since 2016. My goal is to build intelligent systems that solve real problems using language and machine learning models. I translate foundational methods from computer science, computational linguistics, and natural language processing into useful tools and products designed to augment human capabilities with AI.

About

Over the past ten years, I have had the opportunity to work in Mexico, Germany, and the UK, gaining international experience with globally distributed software engineering teams. I have worked with industry and R&D teams across a range of NLP areas, including search and retrieval, dialogue systems, document understanding, and model evaluation. I have contributed to organizations including Chubb, ScaDS.AI, DFKI, AT&T, Globant, Amadeus IT Group, and Sngular.

My background is in computer science. I hold a BSc in Computer Science from the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), an MSc in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Edinburgh, and a PhD in Computer Science from Bielefeld University, with research conducted at DFKI. During my doctorate, I focused on enhancing question banks with NLP, including generating, storing, retrieving, and recommending questions for exam assembly workflows. This work spanned retrieval, ranking, evaluation, and generation methods for domain-specific knowledge systems, reflecting my broader interest in turning language technologies into reliable tools.
