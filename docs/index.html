Hey there 🌵

Hi, I'm Alonso, an NLP researcher at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI). I research and develop educational applications using NLP, working together with industry and government partners.

About

I have extensive industry experience applying NLP to real-world problems, having worked at ScaDS.AI, AT&T, Globant, Amadeus IT Group, and Sngular. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from Bielefeld University's KML Group and DFKI's Educational Technology Lab, I also earned an MSc in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Edinburgh in the UK and a BSc in Computer Science from UNAM in Mexico.
During my PhD, I advanced research in vocational education and training (VET) in Germany through question banking, which includes generating, storing, retrieving, and recommending questions for exam assembly using natural language processing. My work focused on generative NLP and information retrieval models to support search, retrieval, and automated question generation. I developed methods for estimating question difficulty, retrieval, and exam assembly to improve question banks and exam construction in VET, in collaboration with bfz. I am currently working on smart tutoring systems in partnership with the German Association for Water, Wastewater and Waste (DWA), aimed at strengthening vocational training in wastewater management in Germany.
